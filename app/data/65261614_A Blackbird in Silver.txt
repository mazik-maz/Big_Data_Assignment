A Blackbird in Silver is a novel by Freda Warrington published in 1986. ==Plot summary== A Blackbird in Silver is a novel in which adventurers undertake a quest to defeat an evil creature. ==Reception== Dave Langford reviewed A Blackbird in Silver for White Dwarf #75, and stated that "The writing spins out a huge ever-increasing trilogy. Beam me up, Scotty." ==Reviews== *Review by Pauline Morgan (1986) in Fantasy Review, May 1986 ==References== Category:1986 novels
