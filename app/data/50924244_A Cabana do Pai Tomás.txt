A Cabana do Pai Tomás is a Brazilian telenovela produced and broadcast by TV Globo. It is based on Harriet Beecher Stowe's Uncle Tom's Cabin. It premiered on 7 July 1969 and ended on 28 February 1970, with a total of 205 episodes. It was the sixth "novela das sete" to be aired at the timeslot.
