A California Romance is a 1922 American comedy film directed by Jerome Storm and written by Charles E. Banks. The film stars John Gilbert, Estelle Taylor, George Siegmann, Jack McDonald and C.E. Anderson. The film was released on December 24, 1922, by Fox Film Corporation. ==Cast== *John Gilbert as Don Patricio Fernando *Estelle Taylor as Donna Dolores *George Siegmann as Don Juan Diego *Jack McDonald as Don Manuel Casca *C.E.
