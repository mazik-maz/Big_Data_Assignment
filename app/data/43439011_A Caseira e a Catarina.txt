A Caseira e a Catarina is a very popular Brazilian play written by Ariano Suassuna that was first published in 1962.Ariano Suassuna Obras - Academia Brasileira de Letras ==Plot== The plot has one act. It is about a woman betrayed by her husband. Angry and in sorrow, she makes a deal with the devil and asks him to take her husband and his lover to hell. ==References== Category:1962 plays Category:Comedy plays Category:Brazilian plays Category:Plays by Ariano Suassuna
