A Certain Mr. Jobim is the fourth album by Antônio Carlos Jobim. It was released in 1967 and was number 14 on the US Jazz Albums 1968 year-end chart. ==Track listing== ==Personnel== * Antônio Carlos Jobim – piano, guitar, organ, vocals * Claus Ogerman – arranger/conductor * Dom Um Romão – drums * George Lee and Ray Gilbert – producers ==References== Category:1967 albums Category:Antônio Carlos Jobim albums Category:Albums arranged by Claus Ogerman Category:Warner Records albums Category:Albums conducted by Claus Ogerman Category:Albums produced by Ray Gilbert
