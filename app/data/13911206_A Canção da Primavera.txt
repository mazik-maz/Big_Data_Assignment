A Canção da Primavera is a 1923 Brazilian silent romantic drama film directed by Igino Bonfioli and Cyprien Segur. The film premiered in Rio de Janeiro on 24 July 1923. ==Plot== The film is set in Minas Gerais in the 19th century, as it the story of a powerful farmer Luiz Roldão (Castro Vianna) who attempts to force a marriage between his son Jorge (Odilardo Costa) and Rosita, from the Bento's family, to unite their families for economic reasons.
