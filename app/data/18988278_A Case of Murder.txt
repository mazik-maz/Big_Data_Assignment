A Case of Murder is a 2004 action/crime/thriller film which had its release in South Africa on 2 July 2004. It was based on a true story of a hideous murder, where the victim's body was disposed of in a suitcase. The film was dedicated to the memory of veteran actor Ramalao Makhene, who died shortly after shooting was completed.Credit roll from film ==Plot== Two brothers, together with one of their wives, plot to kill the old man they live with and steal his pension.
