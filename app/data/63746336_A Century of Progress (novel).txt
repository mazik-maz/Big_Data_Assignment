A Century of Progress is a novel by Fred Saberhagen published in 1983. ==Plot summary== A Century of Progress is a novel in which Alan Norlund is recruited into a war that involves time travel to the 1930s. ==Reception== Greg Costikyan reviewed A Century of Progress in Ares Magazine #16 and commented that "A Century of Progress is based on a good idea which is not adequately exploited. As such, it is one of Saberhagen's lesser novels." ==Reviews== * Review by Gene DeWeese (1983) in Science Fiction Review, November 1983 ==References== Category:1983 novels
