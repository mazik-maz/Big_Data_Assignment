A Bit of Tom Jones? is a 2009 Welsh comedy film written, produced and directed by Peter Watkins-Hughes. It was shot on location in Tredegar, Wales, and filmed in the summer of 2007. It was independently financed by community businesses in South Wales Valleys. ==Cast== *Jonny Owen *Roger Evans (actor) *Eve Myles *Matt Berry *John Henshaw *Denise Welch *Margaret John *Geno Washington *Neil Rayment *Stephen Marzella ==Reception== In November 2009, the film received a theatrical run at the Vue cinema chain in Wales. So successful was this run, that Vue hosted a Leicester Square red carpet screening in London. It was released on DVD in April 2010.
