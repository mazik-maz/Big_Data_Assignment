A Cabinet of Curiosities () is a 1619 oil on panel painting of a cabinet of curiosities by the Flemish painter Frans Francken II. It is now in the Royal Museum of Fine Arts, Antwerp, which bought it in 1903 from the Antwerp-based art dealer Joseph Hallyn.Koninklijk Muzeum van Schoone Kunsten. Beschrijvend Catalogus. I.
