A Bill of Divorcement is a 1922 British silent drama film based on Clemence Dane's play A Bill of Divorcement. The film was directed by Denison Clift and stars Constance Binney, Fay Compton and Malcolm Keen. ==Synopsis== ==Cast== * Constance Binney as Sidney Fairfield * Fay Compton as Margaret Fairfield * Malcolm Keen as Hilary Fairfield (played same role in the play) * Henry Victor as Grey Meredith * Henry Vibart as Dr. Aliot * Martin Walker as Kit Pumphery * Fewlass Llewellyn as Reverend Christopher Pumphrey (played same role in the play) * Dora Gregory as Hester Fairfield * Sylvia Young as Bassett ==References== ==External links== * * Category:1922 films Category:British drama films Category:British silent feature films Category:Films directed by Denison Clift Category:1922 drama films Category:British films based on plays Category:Ideal Film Company films Category:Films set in England Category:British black-and-white films Category:Films about divorce Category:1920s English-language films Category:1920s British films Category:Silent drama films
