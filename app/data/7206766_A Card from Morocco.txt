A Card from Morocco is a novel written by author and actor Robert Shaw. It was published in 1969. A Card from Morocco was the final novel in a trilogy, having been preceded by The Flag (1965) and The Man in the Glass Booth (1967). It concerns Arthur Lewis and Patrick Slattery, two drinking companions self- exiled from society, and their various misadventures through Spain as they both engage in bragging and self destructive behavior.
