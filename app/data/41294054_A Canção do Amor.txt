A Canção do Amor is the eleventh album in the live praise and worship series of Contemporary worship music by Diante do Trono. == About the project == In the pre-recording of the A Canção do Amor, the mining group released the Príncipe da Paz album, recorded at the Apoteose Square, in Rio de Janeiro. The album was recorded at Chevrolet Hall in Recife, Pernambuco, on 4 and 5 July 2008. All songs were composed by Ana Paula Valadão.
