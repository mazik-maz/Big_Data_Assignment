A Case at Law is a 1917 American silent western drama film directed by Arthur Rosson and starring Richard Rosson, Pauline Curley and Riley Hatch.Langman p.74 The film's production was supervised by Allan Dwan. ==Cast== * Richard Rosson as Jimmy Baggs * Pauline Curley as Mayme Saunders * Riley Hatch as Dr. Saunders * John T. Dillon as Saloonkeeper Art * Eddie Sturgis as The Lob ==References== ==Bibliography== * Langman, Larry. A Guide to Silent Westerns.
